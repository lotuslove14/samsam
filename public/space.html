Our Philosophy

At SAMSAM – YAO NOI, we operate a Villôtel and Nature Lodge placing utmost priority on the privacy of our guests. We wish our guests to experience the tranquil nature of Yao Noi to its full extent in each Villa and Suite. We place high importance on including local culture and people in our operation and ensuring genuine Yao Noi hospitality to our guests.

Our Villas and Suites come with basic hotel services and breakfast. We have a full menu of additional services provided by SAMSAM Family or SAMSAM Friends which you can customize upon check-in or at any time during your stay.

Our Island

Yao Noi is the smaller Yao island just opposite its bigger brother, Yao Yai, in the Phang Nga Bay. The European founders of the SAMSAM family discovered the island twenty-some years ago and fell in love with its natural beauty, and they still visit regularly. The spectacular scenery combined with the inner beauty of its inhabitants has led to a deep relationship between Yao Noi and its people. This concept formed the base for SAMSAM - The Space, and SAMSAM - Family & Friends: we bring the wonder of nature together with luxury.

Quiet And Tranquil

Yao Noi is a quiet and tranquil island, offering beautiful views of the sea, limestone islands, jungle and exotic animals. Our island is ideal for activities like fishing, bicycling, Thai boxing, rock climbing, authentic Thai cooking, Thai massage, Thai herbal and health classes, yoga and many other things to be discovered.

Villa Cortez is how it all started. Built as a tropical masterpiece with beautiful hand-crafted wooden design, Villa Cortez provides a very open and naturally ventilated living space. The 100+ sq.m house with spacious kitchen almost feels like a master Sala when doors and windows are open. The front of the villa faces beautiful views of Phang Nga Bay and the limestones of Koh Hong and Koh Phak Bia, as well as the more remote formations closer to Krabi. Two spacious bedrooms with terrace, balcony and industrial style bathrooms present a relaxed and welcoming feeling. Every morning brings the opportunity of an unforgetable experience: imagine the sun rising right in front of you, inviting the birds to sing whilst painting brilliant colors accross the sky and the limestone islands. The small bamboo spa area with a private sauna, the 16-meter private pool with large outdoor area, and views of the sea all contribute to the perfection of this unique space. Though all our Villas are all built to blend in with their natural environment, Villa Cortez highlights the epitome of this principle.

Villa TreeTop is our biggest Villa with three sleeping rooms, all facing Phang Nga Bay and its limestone formations. As in all our villas, the sun rises directly in front of you to give you a wonderful morning ambiance every day. Villa TreeTop's location directly on the hill gives the impression of staying in the canopy of the tropical forest, especially in the upper living space and sleeping room. The living space and its large terrace present you with magnificent views of the sea, as well as scenes of local wildlife in nearby tree tops. The huge 18-meter private pool in the back overlooks Phang Nga Bay. With its large outdoor area and spacious wooden deck, it is suitable for poolside relaxation and outdoor events. Villa TreeTop provides the perfect experience of modern luxury amidst the wonders of the natural environment.
