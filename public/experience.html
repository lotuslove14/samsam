Our Food

At SAMSAM we are great food lovers and we appreciate authenticity of each location we do sample it. Locally grown product of highest quality and a profound knowledge of preparation techniques as well as a deep emotional bond to the final result on your plate are essential ingredients of our food philosophy.

We do offer basic meals and snacks in our Resort at the Valley View Room and around the Valley Pool area or abundant arrangements of lunch or dinner in your own Space. We do work with experienced Chefs with extensive knowledge on the food, the ingredients, the herbs and spices of their own homes as well as of Southern Thailand. We do source most of our ingredients locally as much as possible from local fishermen or Organic Farms (please see The Water Land)

We offer arrangements within our menus of:

Thai Thai Cuisine

Very authentic Thai cuisine including local dishes, herbal drinks and dishes with local organic rice and ingredients. For real authentic Thai Food lovers. Prepared by the one of the best Chef of Thailand.

My Thai Cuisine

Authentic Thai and partly Fusion Cuisine prepared for those palates with less desire for the intense flavors, herbs and spices. Prepared by top Thai Chefs with experience in cooking for foreign guests.
