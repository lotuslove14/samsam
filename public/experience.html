Our Food

At SAMSAM we are great food lovers and we appreciate the authenticity of our dining experience. Locally grown product of the highest quality, a profound knowledge of preparation techniques, and a deep emotional commitment to the final result are essential ingredients of our food philosophy.

We offer basic meals and snacks at the Valley View Room and around the Valley Pool area, as well as arrangements of lunch or dinner in your own space. We work with experienced chefs who have extensive knowledge of the food, ingredients and flavors of their home cuisines as well as those of Southern Thailand. As much as possible, we source our ingredients locally, from resident fishermen and Organic Farms.

Our Menus offer:

Thai Thai Cuisine

Very authentic southern Thai cuisine including local dishes, herbal drinks and locally sourced ingredients. For real authentic Thai food lovers. Prepared by the one of the best chefs in Thailand.

My Thai Cuisine

Still authentic; select milder and internationally popular Thai dishes prepared for those palates with less desire for the intensity of the herbs and spices in Thai Thai Cuisine. Prepared by top Thai chefs with experience in cooking for foreign guests.

Our Body, Mind and Soul

In our view, Yao Noi is one of the most captivating places on earth, an ideal setting for combining the contemplation of body in physical movement with spiritual meditative practice. As many members of our SAMSAM Family have long developed deep connections with varied techniques, we are glad to share our experiences with you and help you try an Asian way of inner retrospection.

We will also provide class and seminar participants with extra samples of our Hautschild–Skin Systems product range to support the inner regeneration effect with unique topical external treatments developed by one of our sister companies.

For guests that are seeking contemplation, our SAMSAM Team can arrange the following:

Kyudo classes and seminars

Yoga classes

Tai Chi classes and seminars

Thai traditional massage and oil massage

It is possible for your group or family to rent our Japanese Dojo for such classes to grant a spiritual Japanese atmosphere to your sessions.
