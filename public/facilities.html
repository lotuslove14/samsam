The Facilities

As everyone in Our Team has found Yao Noi to be one of the few magic places the world is offering and one discovers from time to time on some journey we have developed some unique facilities. We try to let our guests have options how to relax or develop their Body, their Mind and their Soul while staying with us. We therefore offer some very special and some common guided or independent ways to train and use one of our Facilities.

Our Japanese Dojo

Originally built for Japanese Archery (Kyudo) our Dojo is multifunction and houses many Asiatic techniques to develop your body and mind. It is certainly ideal for Kyudo groups to rent for seminars but can also be used for guided trainings or seminars in the arts of Kendo, Tai Chi, Yoga, any form of meditative practice and even Western Archery. It is a typical Kyudo Dojo built under the advice of Sadayuki Sakane. As such it is a spiritual place with rather Japanese atmosphere and complementing tropical garden elements. Due to the beauty of this Space we have built in four Suites including some Japanese elements.

Our Western Gym

As one of the few resorts we provide fully equipped gym mainly for cardiac as well muscle or calisthenics training sessions with dumbbells, medicine balls or just your own body. Individual or group classes can be organized for the above as well as for Yoga or other fitness sessions.

Our Valley Pool

On top of the individual pools of all our prime villas, we do offer a common pool with fantastic views over the tropical valley gardens right next to the Gym as well as facing our Japanese Dojo.

Our Valley View Room

On top of our Gym we offer a large Space, inside and outside, overlooking the Valley jungle to serve you with small snacks and dishes as well as drinks during your afternoon to evening stays in the common pool area. On occasion the Valley View Room will be used for functions, meetings or dinner events.
