The Facilities

Everyone on our Team has found Yao Noi to be one of the most magical places in the world, and we have drawn from our own experiences and journeys to develop our unique facilities. We provide our guests with varied opportunites to relax or develop their Body, Mind and Soul while staying with us. We therefore offer special guided or independent ways to train in our facilities.

Our Japanese Dojo
(expected opening 2018)

Originally built for Japanese Archery (Kyudo), our Dojo is multifunctional and houses many Asiatic techniques to develop your body and mind. It is certainly ideal for Kyudo groups to rent for seminars but can also be used for guided trainings or seminars in the arts of Kendo, Tai Chi, Yoga, any form of meditative practice and even Western Archery. It is a typical Kyudo Dojo built under the advice of Sadayuki Sakane. As such, it is a spiritual place with a calming atmosphere and complementing tropical garden elements.

Our Western Gym

We provide a fully equipped gym for cardio as well as muscle or calisthenics training. We provide dumbbells, medicine balls and space for body-weight exercises. Individual or group classes can be organized for the above as well as for Yoga or other fitness sessions.

Our Valley Pool

In addition to the private pools of all our prime villas, we offer a common pool with fantastic views over the tropical valley gardens.

Our Valley View Room

Guests also have the use of our Valley View Room, overlooking the Valley jungle. We serve small snacks and dishes as well as drinks during your afternoon or evening stays in the common pool area. On occasion, the Valley View Room can be used for functions, meetings or dinner events.
